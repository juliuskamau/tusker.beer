TUSKER
cider

Tusker Premium Cider is an innovative product that was launched into the market in November, 2016 as the first ever hard apple cider made in Kenya. It sets the benchmark for what a Cider from Kenya is. Tusker Premium Cider is created from the fresh juice of the finest quality apples. Delivering a delectable rich fruit flavour and sharp crisp finish that is lightly carbonated, super refreshing and with a subtle natural sweetness, it offers fresh flavour with an authentic story. Tusker Premium Cider is for those who seek adventure in taste and experiences, those who are unashamedly different and who seek to push the limits. Those who take the party with them wherever they may be. The crisp, zingy, sweet and refreshing taste is just the taste they need to take their adventure to the next level. Tusker premium apple cider is 4.5% ABV, available in 500ml clear glass bottles for 140 KES. Best served chilled.
